What is this?

We love EntryBoss but we struggled to keep track of upcoming races across different clubs. This site aggregates race information from EntryBoss and attempts to presents them in a more discoverable format.

We'll remember your favourite clubs and show you every posted event so you won't miss a thing. Click through to the event page to register directly on EntryBoss.
